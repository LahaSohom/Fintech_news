# Skill: Publish Fintech News

## Purpose
Prepare processed fintech news articles for Streamlit frontend display.

## Steps

### Step 1 — Prepare Final Output
Create a clean article structure for frontend rendering.

### Step 2 — Organize Articles
Ensure articles are sorted by importance score.

### Step 3 — Send To Frontend
Return processed articles to Streamlit UI.

### Step 4 — Display Top 5
Display only the highest ranked fintech news stories.

## Rules
- Clean formatting
- No duplicate articles
- Display trusted source names
- Maintain readability

# Skill: Summarize Fintech News

## Purpose
Generate concise AI-powered summaries for fintech news articles.

## Steps

### Step 1 — Read Article Content
Analyze:
- title
- summary
- source

### Step 2 — Generate Summary
Create a professional 2-line summary.

### Step 3 — Preserve Accuracy
Ensure no hallucinated information is added.

### Step 4 — Return Structured Output
Return:
- title
- ai_summary
- source
- importance_score

## Rules
- Maximum 2 lines
- Professional tone
- No fake information
- Keep financial accuracy
- Avoid repetition

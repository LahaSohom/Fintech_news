# Skill: Rank Fintech News

## Purpose
Identify and rank the top 5 most important fintech news articles.

## Ranking Factors
- Global financial impact
- AI innovation
- Banking relevance
- Market influence
- Funding significance
- Regulatory importance
- Cybersecurity relevance
- Digital payments impact

## Steps

### Step 1 — Analyze Importance
Assign an importance score from 1 to 10.

### Step 2 — Evaluate Business Impact
Check how significantly the news affects fintech industries globally.

### Step 3 — Sort Articles
Sort articles by highest importance score.

### Step 4 — Select Top 5
Return the five most impactful fintech stories.

## Rules
- Prioritize global relevance
- Prioritize innovation
- Avoid clickbait articles
- Prefer factual reporting
